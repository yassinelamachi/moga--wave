Why Essaouira is Special

Discover the unique charm of our coastal paradise that makes it the perfect destination for surfing and yoga

Perfect Winds

Consistent Alizée trade winds create ideal conditions for surfing year-round, making it a world-renowned wind and surf destination.

Beautiful Beaches

Long sandy beaches with various breaks suitable for all skill levels, from gentle waves for beginners to challenging swells for experts.

Mild Climate

Pleasant temperatures throughout the year with over 300 days of sunshine, perfect for outdoor activities in any season.

Rich Culture

UNESCO World Heritage site with centuries of history, amazing food, vibrant local culture, and a welcoming community spirit.
